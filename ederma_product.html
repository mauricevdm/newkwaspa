Description

Bioderma Photoderm Brume Invisible SPF 50 is a high-protection, ultra-lightweight sun mist that offers broad-spectrum UVA/UVB protection with a refreshing, non-sticky finish. Designed for effortless application on the face and body, this invisible mist requires no rubbing, making it ideal for reapplication throughout the day—even over makeup. Powered by Cellular Protection Technology with Vitamin E, it defends the skin against oxidative stress and reinforces the skin’s natural defences to combat premature ageing and sun-related skin damage. The Dermshield lipidic complex mimics the skin’s natural barrier, forming a protective film that enhances sun resistance and improves barrier function. Water-resistant, silicon-free, non-comedogenic, and suitable for all skin types, including sensitive skin.
